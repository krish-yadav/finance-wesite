Our goal is to make customers feel they’re financially secured

ThePayLead is cloud-based business services platform dedicated to helping Entrepreneurs as well as Households easily start and grow their businesses and their personal requirements, at an affordable cost. Also, we are trying to help the employed/unemployed to get an extra income by referring the clients, who are their surrounding needy people only. It is a WIN-WIN situation. We started in October 2014 with the mission of serving the clients as well as providing a source for earning extra income to the referrals. We have since helped start and operate many of businesses by offering a range of business services.

Quality and reliability

In 2018, we launched a range of services to help families easily navigate the legal, tax and regulatory regime in India. Anyone can now earn income by referring their relatives or friends or even themselves through the ThePayLead platform. Our range of services are Accounts & Audit, Income tax, GST, Company/Firm Incorporation, Loans – Home, Mortgage, Business, Educational, Personal; Project Reports, Share Market, Insurance – Life, Medical, General; Real Estate –Independent house, Plot, Flat, Agricultural Land; Jewellery – Gold, Silver, Diamond Embedded; and any other service as per your wish.
